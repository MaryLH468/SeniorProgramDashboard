ℹ️ How This Hub Works
A guide for all senior program staff.
The two-layer approach
1
This hub holds all fixed content — VCAA structures, subject outcomes, assessments, pedagogy, NCCD advice. Updated by the hub owner when curriculum changes.
2
Microsoft 365 tools hold all live, collaborative content — This Week notes, YP progress, key dates, staff contacts. Updated by designated staff daily.
3
Paste your links once — go to each Live section in this hub, paste your Microsoft 365 URL, and save. The Open button will always take staff straight there.
4
Share this hub URL with your senior team via Teams. They bookmark it as their daily starting point. No more hunting for links.

Setting up your links — step by step
1. Go to each Live — Microsoft 365 section in the sidebar (This Week, YP Progress, Key Dates, Staff Contacts).
2. In each section, paste the URL of your Microsoft 365 tool into the link field and click Save.
3. The Open ↗ button will then take staff directly to that tool every time.
4. Share this hub URL with your team via Teams — they bookmark it and use it as their daily starting point.
5. When you need to update the hub itself (e.g. new VCAA requirements, new subject info), download the updated file from Claude, upload it to GitHub, and it updates for everyone automatically.

🧠 Pedagogy Resources
Paste your internal Canvas page links below. Click Open to go directly to each resource.
Cognitive Load Theory — John Sweller
Distinguishes intrinsic, extraneous and germane cognitive load. Key for designing low-load tasks for our YP with diverse needs.
Principles of Instruction — Barak Rosenshine
Includes OTR within explicit instruction. Highlights guided practice and checking for understanding as core to effective teaching.
Formative Assessment — Dylan Wiliam
Frames checking for understanding as a core component of formative assessment. Emphasises responsive teaching based on evidence of learning.
Explicit Instruction — Archer & Hughes
Framework for clear, direct instruction with worked examples, guided practice and independent work. Essential for our diverse learners.
Trauma-Informed & Strength-Based Practice
Guides for trauma-aware teaching, strength-based framing, and low-anxiety remote learning environments for our YP.
